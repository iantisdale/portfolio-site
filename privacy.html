Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains what information Subnet Trainer collects and how it is used.

Privacy Policy

Subnet Trainer does not require account creation and does not collect personal information such as names, passwords, or precise location data.

The app uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash reports and diagnostic information including:

  • Device model
  • Android version
  • App version
  • Crash logs and diagnostic information

This information is used solely to improve app stability, identify bugs, and improve the user experience.

Crash reporting services are provided by Google Firebase Crashlytics.
