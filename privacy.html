Subnet Trainer • Privacy • Disclosure

Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains what information Subnet Trainer collects and how it is used.

Information Collection

Subnet Trainer does not require account creation and does not collect personal information such as:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Passwords
  • Precise location information

Local Data Storage

Subnet Trainer stores learning progress locally on the device in order to support features such as:

  • Experience points (XP)
  • Statistics and accuracy tracking
  • Best streak records
  • Review mistakes functionality
  • Weak areas tracking

This information remains stored locally on the device and is not transmitted to external servers.

Crash Reporting and Diagnostics

The application uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash reports and diagnostic information including:

  • Device model
  • Android version
  • Application version
  • Crash logs and diagnostics

This information is used solely to:

  • Improve application stability
  • Identify software bugs
  • Improve user experience

Crash reporting services are provided through Google Firebase Crashlytics.
